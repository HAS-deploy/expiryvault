What ExpiryVault is

ExpiryVault is a personal reminder tool that helps you track things that expire — passports, licenses, insurance, warranties, memberships, and similar. It does not provide legal, financial, or professional advice, and it is not a substitute for your own records or for official renewal channels.

Your responsibility

ExpiryVault stores data you enter and schedules the reminders you configure. You are responsible for entering correct expiration dates and renewing your documents on time. We do not guarantee the app will fire reminders — iOS may defer, coalesce, or drop local notifications based on device state. Treat ExpiryVault as a helpful second check, not your only reminder.

Subscriptions and in-app purchases

Plus Monthly and Plus Yearly are auto-renewing subscriptions. Plus Lifetime is a one-time non-consumable purchase. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Auto-renewing subscriptions renew within 24 hours prior to the end of each period at the same price, unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
